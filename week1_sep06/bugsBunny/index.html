Bugs Bunny

About Bugs Bunny

  • Bugs Bunny is a cartoon character who is a rabbit who can talk. He is funny and never afraid. Bugs likes to munch on a carrot while being funny and cracking lame jokes
  • Bugs Bunny always says "What's up, Doc?" when he meets his friends or enemies
  • Bugs has a girlfriend Lola Bunny who is always mad at him for ditching her. She still loves him.
